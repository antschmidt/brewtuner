# Brew Tuner — Support

Brew Tuner is a coffee dial-in app for tracking beans, grinders, brew methods, and the brews you make with them. This page covers common questions and how to get help.

## Getting started

After installing the app, you'll see a coffee bean icon and a "Log a brew" button on the Home tab. Before logging your first brew, take a moment to:

1. Open the **Library** tab and tap **Beans → +** to add the coffee you're currently brewing
2. Verify your **Grinder** is in the library (a handful of common grinders are seeded by default — add yours if it isn't there)
3. Pick a **Brew method** that matches what you're brewing today

Then tap **Log a brew** on Home, fill in the form, and save. You'll land on the brew detail page with an **Adjust &amp; re-brew** button — tap it when you want to try the same brew with a small tweak.

## Common questions

**Where does my data live?**
On your device. Brew Tuner stores everything in a private SQLite database. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

**How do I move my data to a new phone?**
Settings → Backup &amp; restore → Export backup. Save the file somewhere (iCloud Drive, Google Drive, AirDrop to your new device). On the new device, install Brew Tuner, then Settings → Backup &amp; restore → Choose backup file → Restore.

**My grinder has fractional or negative settings (like 1Zpresso). Does that work?**
Yes. When adding a grinder, set the step size to whatever matches your dial (0.1 for fine adjustments, 0 for stepless) and enable "Allows negative settings" if applicable.

**Can I measure in ounces and Fahrenheit?**
Yes. Settings → Units lets you independently choose grams or ounces for dose and yield, and °C or °F for temperature. You can mix freely — e.g. grams for grounds, ounces for liquid output, °F for water temperature.

**How do I see all my brews on one specific bean?**
Open any brew on that bean, tap the bean row at the top of the detail screen, and you'll see a per-bean dial-in view with brews grouped by (grinder, brew method) and a best-brew callout.

**Why is there no sync to iCloud / a cloud backup?**
By design — keeping the app account-free and server-free means there's nothing for an outage or company change to break. The Backup &amp; restore feature gives you the same end-state with full control over where your data goes.

**The app crashes / behaves unexpectedly.**
Try fully closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, email me with what you were doing when it happened (steps to reproduce help a lot).
